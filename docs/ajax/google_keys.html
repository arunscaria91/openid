How to get Google OAuth key?

STEP 1:

Login to Google account and navigate to https://accounts.google.com/ManageDomains



STEP 2:

Follow the instructions and verify the domain added using any of the method listed.



STEP 3:

If verification succeeds then success page will be displayed. Click continue;



STEP 4:

Now you can see the details about the domain you just registered. Note down the consumer key and consumer secret key.



STEP 5:

Fill the OpenID settings page with Google OAuth key. and you are done!!
It's time to login !!
