How to get Twitter App keys?

STEP 1:

Login to Twitter Developer site and navigate to https://dev.twitter.com/apps/new



STEP 2:

Enter an valid App name, description, website and the callback URL and submit the form.



STEP 3:

Now you can see the details about the app you just created. Note down the consumer key and consumer secret key.



STEP 4:

Fill the OpenID settings page with twitter app key and secret key. and you are done!! Select the option "Enable twitter email confirmation" to confirm emails registered under twitter. (Recommended)
It's time to login !!
