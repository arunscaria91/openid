How to get Facebook App keys?

STEP 1:

Login to FaceBook Developer site and navigate to https://developers.facebook.com/apps



STEP 2:

Enter an valid App name and submit the form.



STEP 3:

Complete the captcha to create FaceBook App.



STEP 4:

Fill the form with contact mail id, upload the app icon. Also add the site address where the app need to redirect user.

Note down the facebook app key and secret key. These keys are to be configured in OpenID settings page to setup the facebook OpenID.



STEP 5:

Fill the OpenID settings page with facebook app key and secret key. and you are done!!
It's time to login !!
